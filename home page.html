About Us

Who We Are

The most integrated automotive e-commerce platform in Southeast Asia is called CARMOTOR. By redesigning and improving the automobile purchasing and selling experience, we hope to digitalize the used car market in the area.
From vehicle inspection to ownership transfer to finance, CARMOTOR offers end-to-end solutions to customers and used car dealers, providing a reliable, practical, and effective service.

Vision

To build the most reliable automobile ownership ecosystem possible using data and technology

Mission

To provide our customers comfort during every stage of owning a pre-owned car, from purchase to maintenance
